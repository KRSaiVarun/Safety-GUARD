### Before you push

One important thing: **rotate your exposed credentials**. In this conversation you shared what appear to be Twilio credentials. Before making your repository public:

* Regenerate the **Twilio Auth Token** in the Twilio Console.
* Change any exposed database passwords.
* Keep only placeholders in `.env.example`.
