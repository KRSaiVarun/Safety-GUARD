## The main Twilio issue

From the error:

> **Twilio could not find a Channel with the specified From address (63007)**

Your environment currently contains:

```
TWILIO_WHATSAPP_NUMBER=8618266736
```

This is **not** a valid Twilio WhatsApp sender.

For the Twilio Sandbox, it should be:

```
TWILIO_WHATSAPP_NUMBER=whatsapp:+14155238886
```

or, if you're using a WhatsApp Business account, it must be your **approved Twilio WhatsApp sender**, not your personal phone number.

Until that is corrected, WhatsApp messages will never be sent, and the rest of the emergency flow will not complete properly.
